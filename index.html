The Porob Decorators Story

Based out of the heart of Goa, Porob Decorators is a premier, trusted event infrastructure rental house. Owned and personally managed by Prasad Shambhu Porob and family, our business has built a foundational reputation for dependability, premium stock maintenance, and flawless timely execution.

We understand that every celebration—be it an intimate traditional wedding, a grand corporate seminar, a lively beachside cocktail hour, or a sacred religious ceremony—demands pristine event rentals. Our closely-knit family operations ensure personalized attention to every client request, helping event planners and families bring their visions to life flawlessly all over Goa.
